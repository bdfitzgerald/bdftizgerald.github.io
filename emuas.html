EMUAS Members App

Project Details

East Midlands Universities Air Squadron is a RAF reservist squadron based in the East Midlands area. Their mission is to recruit officers into the RAF, and improve the perception of the Armed Forces in general. They operate as a University Society affiliated with teh RAF to provide unique training opportunities and insights into the RAF. With all the activities they participate in it is essentail to be able to manage these in a simple way. Having been a previous member I volunteered to build a members area and corresponding mobile app to aid them. The members area consists of a forum and messaging service, similar to a facebook group, but with more options.
